Controls;

P- Toggle showing mouse cursor
B- Toggle showing bounds
F1- Enable face culling
F2- Disable face culling
F3- Disable wireframe mode
F4- Enable wireframe mode

Camera-
Mouse- Move around to pan
Arrow Keys- Look around
WASD- Move around

Inventory-
1- Hold first item
2- Hold second item
3- Hold third item
4- Hold fourth item
5- Hold fifth item
[LEFTCLICK] - Pickup item
[RIGHTCLICK] - Put down item

Lights-
Z- Switch on lighting
X- Disable lighting

I- Hold down to show shopping list
